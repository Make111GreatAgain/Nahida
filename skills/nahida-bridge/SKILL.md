# Nahida Bridge

Read `vault/00_System/NAHIDA_SPEC.md` before writing.

## Workflow

1. Identify the two domains or topics being connected.
2. Gather supporting sources, claims, concepts, or maps.
3. Choose `relation_type`: `application`, `analogy`, `dependency`, `tension`, `translation`, or `shared_pattern`.
4. Create or update a bridge note under `06_Bridges/`.
5. Include both transferable concepts and limits of the relationship.
6. Queue low-confidence bridges for review.

## Boundaries

- May write `06_Bridges/` and review queues.
- Must not import sources or rewrite concept notes directly.
